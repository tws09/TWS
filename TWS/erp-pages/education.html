Key Capabilities

  • ✅ Gradebook: Configurable grading scales (GPA, CGPA, weighted).
  • ✅ Transcripts: One-click generation of official, watermarked PDF transcripts.
  • ✅ Attendance: Biometric integration and mobile app marking for teachers.

Learning Management (LMS)

The Digital Campus

A unified learning experience platform (LXP) that supports blended, synchronous, and self-paced learning.

Key Capabilities

  • ✅ Live Classes: Embedded Zoom/Teams with auto-attendance and recording.
  • ✅ Proctoring: AI-based browser lockdown and gaze tracking for exams.
  • ✅ Gamification: Badges, leaderboards, and certificates for course completion.
  • ✅ Resource Library: Unlimited storage for lecture notes, videos, and SCORM packs.
