Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about security, onboarding, and support.

How secure is student data?
Student data is protected with encryption at rest and in transit. We're FERPA, GDPR, SOC 2 Type II, and ISO 27001 certified. Multi-tenant architecture ensures logical data isolation between institutions. Regular security audits and automated backups protect your data.
How long does onboarding take?
Most institutions are up and running within 2-4 weeks. We provide dedicated onboarding specialists, data migration tools, and comprehensive training. For single-campus deployments, you can start with core modules in as little as 7 days.
What kind of support do you provide?
We offer 24/7 technical support via email, chat, and phone. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers, priority support, and custom training sessions. Our knowledge base and video tutorials help your team get the most out of the platform.
Can we integrate with existing systems?
Yes. Our RESTful APIs and pre-built connectors allow integration with payment gateways, email systems, library systems, and other educational software. We provide integration documentation and developer support to ensure seamless connectivity.
What's the pricing model?
Pricing is based on the number of students and selected modules. We offer flexible plans for single campuses, multi-campus groups, and enterprise networks. Contact us for a customized quote based on your institution's specific needs and scale.
Does it work for both K-12 and universities?
Absolutely. Our modular platform adapts to any educational institution. K-12 schools focus on classroom management and parent communication, while universities leverage research administration and advanced analytics. The same platform, configured for your needs.
